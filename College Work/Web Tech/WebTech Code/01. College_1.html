About Galgotias College of Engineering & Technology (GCET)

Established: Founded in 1999 by Suneel Galgotia, it offers courses in various subjects up to post-graduation and serves as the corporate headquarters of Galgotias Educational Institutions (GEI).

History

Founded with a mission to advance technical education, Galgotias College of Engineering & Technology (GCET) has grown into a renowned institution, initially offering two engineering programs and now covering multiple disciplines across undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, foster research, and encourage industry collaboration. XYZ College stands for integrity, inclusivity, and a commitment to high standards in all endeavors.

Facilities

  • Library: Thousands of books, journals, and digital resources available.
  • Modern Labs: Hands-on facilities for computer science, electronics, and more.
  • Hostels: Safe and comfortable accommodation for students.
  • Sports Complex: Cricket, football, basketball courts, and a fully equipped gym.
